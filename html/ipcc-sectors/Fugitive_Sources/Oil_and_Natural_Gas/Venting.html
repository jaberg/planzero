Understanding Venting

Reducing Venting Emissions

Critical Success Factors

  • Reduce the use of natural gas to drive pneumatic equipment
  • Reduce the frequency and severity of leaks from all gas-handling equipment
  • Reduce emissions of solution gas and case gas from oil extraction sites
  • Reduce the number of oil and gas extraction sites
  • Reduce the amount of transmission and processing of natural gas
  • Reduce emissions from abandoned wells and penetrated gas-bearing formations
  • Reduce venting of gas during equipment maintenance activities
  • Reduce exposure of oil sands to open atmosphere
  • Reduce exposure of oil sands tailings and settling ponds to open atmosphere
  • Reduce the venting of acid gas, which comprises mostly carbon dioxide

Barriers

  • Natural gas leaks are not generally easy to detect, or locate
  • Historically, pneumatic equipment that vents depressurized natural gas was standard technology
