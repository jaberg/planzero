Understanding Venting

Reducing Venting Emissions

Critical Success Factors

  • Reduce the number of oil and gas wells
  • Reduce the venting of casing gas from oil and gas wells
  • Reduce the use of natural gas to drive pneumatic equipment
  • Reduce the venting of gas during equipment maintenance activities
  • Reduce the release of acid gas, which comprises mostly carbon dioxide
  • Reduce exposure of oil sands to open atmosphere
  • Reduce exposure of oil sands tailings and settling ponds to open atmosphere

Barriers

  • Oil and gas continue to receive enormous market demand.
  • Comparable oil and gas operators in the US are scaling up under policies of the Trump administration
  • Historically, pneumatic equipment that vents depressurized natural gas was standard technology.
  • It is usually cheaper to venting something than to store it.
  • Small remote facilities may have no convenient source of electricity to drive non-pneumatic equipment.
  • There is no practical technology to prevent methane escape from oil sands mining surfaces or tailings ponds.
