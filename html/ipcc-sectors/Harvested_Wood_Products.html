Critical Success Factors

  • Increase the size of HWP carbon pools: harvest more timber, especially for long-lived products
  • Slow the emissions: delay or prevent the decay of harvested wood products
N.B. Reducing the volume of harvested wood products would not reduce national emissions, the carbon would simply remain in the Forest Land sector.
